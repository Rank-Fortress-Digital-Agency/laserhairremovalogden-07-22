Is Laser Hair Removal Right For You?


Laser hair removal is a popular cosmetic procedure that many people are considering. But is it right for you

Laser hair removal uses a concentrated light beam to target the pigment in your hair follicles. The heat from the laser breaks down the hair follicle, causing the hair to fall out.

The procedure is most effective on dark hair and lighter skin but can be done on all skin types and hair colors. It typically takes multiple sessions to see results, which are not permanent.
